About Hydrospect

Hydrospect is a research-led technical consultancy specializing in underwater remote sensing. We provide the expertise required to bridge the gap between complex raw data and actionable environmental insights.

With a Cat A accredited hydrographic education and experience in both the professional and research domains, our focus lies in achieving technical certainty as a means for delivering value. We support academic institutions, environmental NGOs, and software developers in ensuring the precision of subsea data through independent verification and specialised desk-based assessments.
